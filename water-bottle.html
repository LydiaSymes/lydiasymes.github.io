Sea Life Water Bottle

For my final project of first year university, I worked in the area of surface design.
The aim was to create 3 x colourful and eye catching illustrated water bottle designs.


The purpose of these illustrations is to raise awareness of endangered sea life that are currently being affected by plastic pollution in the sea. Each design focuses on a sea life creature, seahorses, turtles and the coral reef. Our target audience for these bottle designs are young students aged 16-24 (in particular, higher education such as college and university students) as reusable water bottles are popular amongst this demographic when going to their studies.
